Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

Thank you for using Ormi. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the choices you have regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

Ormi stores your data locally on your Android device by default using an offline Room SQLite database.

If you choose to sign in and enable cloud backup or synchronization, the following information may be stored securely in Google Cloud Firestore:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (Photo URL)
  • Google account User ID (UID)
  • Custom habits
  • Habit completion history
  • Habit streaks and statistics
  • Tasks and to-do lists
  • Task completion status
  • Weight logs
  • Sleep logs
  • Protein intake logs
  • App preferences and settings
  • Monk Mode settings
  • Blocked app list
  • Synchronization timestamps

We do not collect your contacts, messages, photos, or location unless explicitly stated in future versions of the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Synchronize your data across your devices.
  • Restore your information after reinstalling the app.
  • Provide habit tracking, task management, and productivity features.
  • Improve app stability and user experience.
  • Provide customer support when requested.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with advertisers.

3. Permissions Used

Ormi may request the following Android permissions:

  • Usage Access – Detect the currently open application for Monk Mode.
  • Accessibility Service (optional) – Improve app blocking functionality on supported devices.
  • Display Over Other Apps (Overlay) – Display the focus screen when blocked apps are opened.
  • Ignore Battery Optimizations – Keep reminders and focus sessions working reliably.
  • Notifications – Send reminders and productivity notifications.

Permissions are requested only when needed and can be revoked at any time through your device settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Ormi uses trusted Google services, including:

  • Google Firebase Authentication
  • Google Cloud Firestore
  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing (for subscriptions, if applicable)

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Cloud data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and stored securely using Google Cloud services.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

You remain in control of your data. You can delete your local data, cloud-synchronized data, or your account through the app settings where available. You may also request deletion by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

Ormi is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated revision date.
