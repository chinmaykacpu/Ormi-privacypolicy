Delete Your Ormi Account

At Ormi, we respect your privacy and give you full control over your data. You can permanently delete your account and associated cloud data at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Ormi app.
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  3. Read the confirmation message.
  4. Confirm deletion.

What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your Google Sign-In account is disconnected from Ormi.
  • Your cloud data stored in Firebase Firestore is permanently deleted.
  • Your habits, tasks, weight logs, protein logs, sleep logs, settings, and other synced data are permanently removed.
  • Locally stored data on your device is removed when you uninstall the app or clear the app's storage.
